AI/ML Implementation

AI Models Deployed

1. Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Base: mBERT (Multilingual BERT) fine-tuned on 100K LPG safety conversations across 11 languages. Achieves 94% intent classification accuracy.

2. Computer Vision

Custom CNN with ResNet-50 backbone trained on 65,000 images. Targets 98% genuine detection, 95% fake detection accuracy.

3. Recommendation Engine

Hybrid system combining collaborative filtering, content-based filtering, and context-aware scoring for personalized content delivery.

4. Anomaly Detection

Isolation Forest + LSTM models for fraud detection including duplicate QR scans, timeline inconsistencies, and blockchain hash mismatches.
